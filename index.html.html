About Me

✦

Hello, I'm Ananya Anuska — a poet and artist who finds meaning in the spaces between words and lines between shapes.

I have always believed that creativity is not a talent but a practice — a daily act of noticing, feeling, and translating the world into something that can be held.

This space is my journal, my gallery, my letter to the world. I am glad you found it.

— Ananya Anuska
